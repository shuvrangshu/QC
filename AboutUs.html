Why Quick Career ?

Quick Career Computer Academy is a sister concern of M/S Quick Distribution that was established in 1196. Our aim was to establish Computer Training Institute for those who don't get chance to have quality training with minimum cost. That's why we have planned to set up Computer Training Institutes to different places of our state for those who need quality training but don't have proper financial support.

We don't believe in goodwill that can be built through publicity. We believe in goodwill that can be built only through hard labour. Our course fees is 50% lower than other centres because we don't pay a huge amount for publicity purpose like others.

We started our institution 16 years ago with 6 courses only, but now the no of courses are 12 and our aim is to serve our students minimum 18 type of courses with a minimum course fees.

We believe in Quality not in publicity
