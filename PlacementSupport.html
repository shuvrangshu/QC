Placement

Now a days to get a job is easy but to maintain a job is too much tuff. So we focus on the quality of a student through which the student can find a job for himself. You just think a bit wisely. can any institute give guarantee of a job to a student? It's nothing but an Ad.

So we are trying to provide such type of quality training by which the student can be abled to get a job easily.

What is our Placement Support?

Phase - 1 : We try to upgrade the syllabus according to market need by which our students can easily compete to any other students of any other institutions.

Phase - 2 : We try to develop our student's communication skill by which he/she can easily face an interview in english. For that reason, we provide our students spoken/communitive english class free of cost.

Phase - 3 : We provide an Interview training also that helps a candidate to crack Technical and Non Technical round of an Interview free of cost.

Our Key Points:

1) Market oriented course syllabus

2) Free Communicative English Class

3) Free Grooming Class

4) Free Interview Training

5) 100% Job Interview
